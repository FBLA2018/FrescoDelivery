Frequently Asked Questions

Package Arrival

Your boxes will arrive within 3 days of ordering.

Cold Orders

If you order something cold, we will contact you to make sure you are in town at time of arrival, and your package will be insulated to remain fresh.

One-Time Package vs Membership

With a membership you receive whichever packages you subscribed to monthly. With a one-time package however, you pay once and receive only one package, unless you choose to purchase it again.

Returns

Fresco delivery has a satisfaction guarantee, so if any package arrives that isn’t right or is damaged send photo evidence to us in the contact form and we will send you another.
